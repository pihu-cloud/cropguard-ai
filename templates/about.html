What is CropGuard AI?

CropGuard AI is a deep-learning web application that uses an ensemble of MobileNetV2 and ResNet50V2 convolutional neural networks trained on the PlantVillage dataset (54,306 images) to detect {{ disease_count }} plant conditions across 5 major crops.

Early disease detection can reduce crop losses by up to 40% and minimize pesticide overuse — helping farmers make faster, smarter, data-driven decisions.

Our Mission

Democratize precision agriculture. Make expert-level crop diagnostics accessible to every farmer — regardless of location or resources.

Enable Real Predictions
  1. Train or download your Keras models with output shape (None, {{ disease_count }})
  2. Place them in the models/ directory as mobilenet.h5 and resnet.h5
  3. Restart the Flask server — models are loaded automatically on startup
models/
  mobilenet.h5   ← MobileNetV2 trained model
  resnet.h5      ← ResNet50V2 trained model
